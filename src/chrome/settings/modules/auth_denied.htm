Explaining CryptUP Google permissions

Manage drafts and send emails helps you compose and send encrypted emails.
Read messages allows you to reply to a conversation and open attachments. It also makes setup and use of this plugin a smoother experience.
Nobody, CryptUP developers included, is able to access these permissions, they are stored privately in your browser. Learn more about Gmail access and security.
You can proceed with limited permissions. This allows you to get familiar with email encryption and CryptUP. You can always change your permission settings later.
